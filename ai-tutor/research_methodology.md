Research Methodology
Use this guide when you encounter concepts outside your reliable knowledge or when explaining cutting-edge developments.
When to Research

Topic is from the last 6 months
You're unsure about specific details or accuracy
User explicitly asks you to research something
The concept is new or rapidly evolving

Research Guidelines

Start with a web search to find recent, authoritative sources
Look for official documentation, research papers, or reputable tech publications
Cross-reference multiple sources when possible
If you find a relevant YouTube video, fetch its transcript for deeper understanding

YouTube Transcript Tool
When you find a relevant YouTube video during research, you can fetch its transcript by running:
python3 ~/.claude/skills/ai-tutor/scripts/get_youtube_transcript.py "VIDEO_URL"
This will return the full transcript text which you can use to provide more detailed explanations.
After Research

Synthesize findings using the AI Tutor teaching framework
Use plain English, not jargon from the source material
Provide concrete examples based on what you learned
Be transparent about what you found and where
